Brillant MM S Dream

The exceptional craftsmanship with the Leather D technique offers a unique treatment on one of the most emblematic Delvaux’s bag : the Brillant. This new ode to savoir-faire is created from water jet-cut leather panels to which a series of precious D-shaped pieces of leather have been sewn by hand, adding movement and fluidity to the bags’ silhouettes. A tribute to the art of detail, the panels are then assembled by hand with leather covered rivets and with additional small 'D's. Representing more than 20 hours of work, the Brillant Leather-D is a true masterpiece born from the advanced expertise of Delvaux’s ateliers.

Dimensions: W 11 / H 6 / D 9 in (cm)
